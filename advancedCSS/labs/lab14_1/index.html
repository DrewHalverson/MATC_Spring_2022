College Credit Classes

Follow the steps below to enroll in classes required for degree or certificate completion. These credits may transfer to other colleges.

  1. LOG IN  to your myMadisonCollege account to access your enrollment dates, your shopping cart, and more.
  2. SEARCH  for the courses you want. You can search classes without logging in, but you cannot enroll without logging in.
  3. CONFIRM  you meet all enrollment requirements by clicking the "Validate" button.
  4. COMPLETE  your enrollment by clicking the "Enroll" button.

If you need help or want to register over the phone or in person, please call or visit the Enrollment Center. You can also make an appointment with an  academic advisor to get help with selecting classes.

Continuing Education Classes

Follow the steps below to enroll in classes for professional development or personal enrichment. These credits do not transfer to other schools.  No application is needed to enroll in continuing education classes.

  1. LOG IN  to your myMadisonCollege account to access your shopping cart.   Create your account or reset your password.
  2. SEARCH  for the courses you want. On the search page, enter the semester and non-degree class type along with the keywords or the five-digit class number. You must log in to add a course to your cart.
  3. CONFIRM  there are no time conflicts or other issues by clicking the “Validate” button.
  4. COMPLETE  your enrollment by clicking the "Enroll" button.

If you need help or wish to register by phone, be sure you have the five-digit class number and call (608) 258-2301, ext. 2.
