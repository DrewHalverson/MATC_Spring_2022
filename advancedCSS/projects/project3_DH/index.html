Fighting hunger and poverty in our local community

Harvest Food Bank operates one of the largest food pantries in the local area. We serve more than 15,000 families, providing over 7 tons of food annually to our community

Food insecurity has always been an issue for many people here and the Covid-19 pandemic has seen it become an even larger issue. We are working tirelessly to ensure that every member of our community has enough to eat on a daily basis.
