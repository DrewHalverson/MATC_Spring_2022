Our Food Pantry

What to Expect

We carry a wide variety of food and other necessities!Our food recovery program provides us with items donated from local grocers as well as ready to eat items from organizations in the area that have on site cafeterias.

How To Register

Registration Details: When you arrive at the pantry, look for one of our registration volunteers wearing yellow vests. They will assist you with registering to receive food and other items based on the size of your family.

Curbside Pickup

COVID-19 Precautions have led us to limit our services to curbside pickup. You will still be able to choose from a variety of items, which will be brought by our volunteers to your vehicle.
